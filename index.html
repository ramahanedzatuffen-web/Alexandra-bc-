Our Story

More Than a Game

Alex Basketball Club is a community-based, registered Non-Profit Company (NPC) based in Alexandra Township, Johannesburg. We provide free, inclusive basketball development, localised school leagues, and tournament opportunities for over 300 young people — from age 10 to young adults. Our 100% volunteer-led model means no child is ever sidelined by financial barriers.

✅ Registered NPC 📍 Alexandra Township, JHB 🆓 Always Free to Join 🙋 100% Volunteer-Led 🏀 300+ Youth Served
🏆
Elite Performance
Cultivating competitive squads that compete at provincial and national levels.
🎓
Educational Pathways
Securing university scholarships in South Africa and abroad for our players.
🏘️
Safe Community Hub
Keeping children off the streets with daily, structured alternatives to street life.
🌱
Holistic Support
Providing tutoring programmes and essential clothing drives alongside sport.

Before: Struggling at school, no routine
After: Grade 12 passed, club captain

Parent Story

Parent of Two Players

Get Involved

How You Can Help

Alex Basketball Club runs entirely on community goodwill and the dedication of our volunteers. Every contribution — whether time, resources, or partnership — goes directly to our youth. Here's how you can make a real difference.

🤝
Corporate Partnership
Align your organisation with authentic grassroots impact in Alexandra Township. We work with partners to create meaningful, tailored community engagement — not just logo placement.
🎽
Donate Equipment
Basketballs, kits, training gear, and transport costs are our biggest recurring needs. Equipment donations go directly to players on the court.
📚
Support Education
Fund tutoring programmes, school partnerships, or help a player access a university scholarship opportunity in South Africa or abroad.
🏆
Sponsor a Tournament
We partner with AYDF to host competitive tournaments. Tournament sponsorship puts your support right at the heart of our most visible community events.
🏘️
Volunteer With Us
Our entire club is volunteer-run. If you have skills in coaching, administration, communications, or logistics — we want to hear from you.
👶
Sponsor a Player
Cover a player's transport, kit, or tournament entry costs. Many of our young athletes travel long distances on public transport to make practice.
Ready to Make a Difference?
We're a registered NPC committed to transparency. Reach out and we'll find a way to work together that fits your capacity — big or small, every gesture changes a life.
