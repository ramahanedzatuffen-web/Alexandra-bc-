Who We Are

About Alex BC

Alex Basketball Club is a community-based, registered Non-Profit Company (NPC) based in Alexandra Township, Johannesburg. We provide free, inclusive basketball development, localised school leagues, and tournament opportunities for over 300 young people — from age 10 to young adults. Our 100% volunteer-led model means no child is ever sidelined by financial barriers.

✅ Registered NPC 📍 Alexandra Township, JHB 🆓 Always Free to Join 🙋 100% Volunteer-Led 🏀 300+ Youth Served

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯 Mission
To transform the lives of underprivileged youth in Alexandra Township by providing a free, inclusive basketball development programme. We foster discipline, promote healthy lifestyles, and teach valuable life skills that empower children to become responsible community members.
🌟 Vision
To be the leading community-driven sports programme in Johannesburg, recognised for using basketball as a powerful tool for social change — producing leaders, scholars, and role models who give back to the community that raised them.
🏀 Empowerment Through Play
Basketball builds the leadership, discipline, and self-belief essential for life success. Every drill, every game, every team huddle teaches skills that go far beyond the court.
🌍 Radical Inclusivity
A safe space open to everyone, regardless of skill level or socioeconomic background. No tryouts. No fees. No barriers. If you want to play, you play.
🏘️ Community Ownership
A completely local initiative where everyone has a voice in our collective growth. Our coaches, managers and leaders all come from and live in the community they serve.
📊 Holistic Growth
We measure success by personal, academic, and mental well-being — not just scoreboard points. A player who passes their exams is as celebrated as one who scores 20 points.

What We Do

Our Core Impact Pillars

🏆
Elite Performance
Cultivating competitive squads that compete at provincial and national levels, giving our players exposure to high-level basketball and the discipline it demands.
🎓
Educational Pathways
Securing university scholarships in South Africa and abroad. We connect academically strong players to opportunities that would otherwise be out of reach.
🏘️
Safe Community Hub
Keeping children off the streets by offering daily, structured alternatives to street life. Training runs Monday to Friday so there is always somewhere safe to be.
🌱
Holistic Support
Providing tutoring programmes and essential clothing drives alongside sport. We support the whole child — not just the athlete.

What We Offer

Our Programmes

We train at No.3 Square Multi-Purpose Centre daily to deliver specialised youth programmes for all ages and skill levels.

🏫
Schools Basketball Program
We partner directly with the local Education District Office to implement an active sports league. The network connects more than 200 students through regular coaching clinics and school leagues.
🦅
Eagles League
Our senior competitive programme enters teams into the Eagles Basketball League, giving players exposure to high-level competition across Gauteng and beyond.
🏘️
Community League
A local league run by Aubrey Mohlala for community members of all ages. Focused on fun, fitness, and keeping the community connected through sport.
👧
Girls Programme
Dedicated U14 Girls training under Coach Kgaugelo Phiri. A safe, empowering space where young women develop confidence, skills, and leadership through basketball.
📚
Tutoring & Life Skills
Academic support and life skills clinics run in partnership with AYDF. Players are supported not just as athletes but as students and community members.
🌱
Clothing Drives
Seasonal clothing drives ensure our players and their families have essential items. No child should miss training because they don't have the right clothing.

Common Questions

FAQ

How do I join Alex Basketball Club?
▼
WhatsApp us first on 082 900 0441 to let us know you're interested, then come down to No.3 Square Sports Grounds any weekday between 4PM and 7PM. Our coaches will welcome you and get you started right away.
Is it really free? Are there any hidden costs?
▼
Yes — completely free. No joining fees, no monthly fees, no kit fees. Alex Basketball Club operates on a 100% volunteer-led model funded by our partners. No child is ever turned away or asked to pay.
What age groups do you cater for?
▼
We welcome young people from 10 years old all the way through to young adults. We have dedicated programmes for U14 Girls, U14 Boys, U23 Men, and a Senior/Community League for older players.
Where do you train?
▼
All training takes place at No.3 Square Sports Grounds, 131-13th Avenue, Alexandra, Johannesburg. We train Monday to Friday from 4PM to 7PM. No appointment needed — just show up.
Does my child need prior experience to join?
▼
Not at all. We welcome complete beginners and experienced players alike. Our coaches assess each player individually and place them in the right programme. Radical inclusivity is one of our core values — everyone is welcome.
