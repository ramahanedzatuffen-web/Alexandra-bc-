Our Story

More Than a Game

Founded in 2015, Alexandra Basketball Club has grown from a small community group into one of the most respected clubs in the region. We compete across all age groups with a shared commitment to excellence, respect, and community.

🏆
Excellence
We train hard and compete harder, always striving to be the best.
🤝
Community
Basketball is a team sport — and so is building a neighbourhood.
🌱
Development
We invest in every player's growth on and off the court.
🔥
Passion
We play with energy, heart, and an unbeatable spirit.

Support Our Mission

Alexandra Basketball Club is a non-profit organisation committed to uplifting youth through sport. We rely on the generosity of partners, donors, and the community to keep our programmes running — free of charge for all our members.

🏀
Equipment & Kit
Help us provide balls, uniforms, and training gear so every young player can participate regardless of their background.
🏟️
Facility Access
Your support helps us secure court time and maintain a safe, welcoming space for all our teams to train and compete.
📚
Youth Development
We run coaching clinics, mentorship, and life-skills programmes that go beyond the game.
✈️
Travel & Tournaments
Donations help our teams travel to regional and national competitions, giving them exposure they couldn't otherwise afford.
🤝
Community Partner
In-kind or small contribution
  • Logo on our website
  • Social media recognition
  • Match-day programme listing
💜
Club Supporter
Regular / ongoing support
  • All Community Partner benefits
  • Courtside banner
  • Logo on training kit
  • Dedicated social media posts
⭐
Founding Partner
Strategic / major partnership
  • All Club Supporter benefits
  • Jersey logo placement
  • PA announcements at games
  • Exclusive partner event invite
  • Featured story on website

All contributions go directly to supporting players and programmes. Contact us to discuss how you can help.
