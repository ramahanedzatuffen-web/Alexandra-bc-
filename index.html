Our Story

More Than a Game

Alexandra Basketball Club is a non-profit organisation based in Alexandra, Johannesburg. Our mission is to develop a viable basketball infrastructure within the townships and eventually nationwide — giving every young person the opportunity to play, grow, and thrive through the sport they love.

🏆
Excellence
We train hard and compete harder, always striving to be the best.
🤝
Community
Basketball is a team sport — and so is building a neighbourhood.
🌱
Development
We invest in every player's growth on and off the court.
🔥
Passion
We play with energy, heart, and an unbeatable spirit.

Get Involved

Support Our Mission

Alexandra Basketball Club is a registered non-profit organisation based in Alexandra, Johannesburg. Our mission is to develop basketball infrastructure in townships across South Africa. Every donation, partnership, and act of support goes directly towards our players and programmes — which are free for all members.

🏀
Equipment & Kit
Help us provide balls, uniforms, and training gear so every young player can participate regardless of their background.
🏟️
Facility Access
Your support helps us secure court time and maintain a safe, welcoming space for all our teams to train and compete.
📚
Youth Development
We run coaching clinics, mentorship, and life-skills programmes that go beyond the game.
✈️
Travel & Tournaments
Donations help our teams travel to regional and national competitions, giving them exposure they couldn't otherwise afford.
🤝
Community Partner
Join us as a Community Partner and help us keep basketball accessible to every young person in Alexandra — regardless of their background.
  • ✓ Your logo on our website & social media
  • ✓ Recognition at match days & events
  • ✓ Direct impact on youth in Alexandra
  • ✓ Personalised partnership — we work with what you can give
